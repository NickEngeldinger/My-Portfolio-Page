About this site

I approached the design and development of this portfolio site with a few goals in mind. I wanted to experiment with various HTML5 and CSS3 techniques and develop a responsive layout that would adapt to appropriately display on a variety of devices.

This site is powered by LESS, Semantic.gs and FlexSlider. The source code for this site and more of my work is located on github. You can also find me on LinkedIn.
